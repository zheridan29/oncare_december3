About Neo Care Philippines

Company Profile

Neo Care Philippines (NCP) is a single proprietorship founded in October 1997 by Lenny Castillo. It is classified as Drug Distributor/Importer by FDA.

It started as a trading company operating in Batangas province selling oral and injectable antibiotics. After years of operation and with the blessing from the Lord, the company is now carrying ten brands of medicines to all regions in Luzon which are all registered under the company's name.

At present NCP is engaged in the importation of finished products for wholesale distribution, promotion, and marketing of drugs and medicines to other pharmaceutical establishments and outlets.

Company Vision

"To be the number one pharmaceutical supplier in the Philippines by selling the highest quality medicines at most affordable price"

Company Mission

"To help Filipino families live happier and healthier and to help in nation building by providing high quality medicine at affordable price, by adhering to the biblical standard of doing business, by giving its employees meaningful work opportunities, by committing to suppliers & investors a lucrative rate of return and by adhering to the government policies and concerns."

FDA Licensed

Classified as Drug Distributor/Importer by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) ensuring regulatory compliance and quality standards.

Nationwide Reach

Serving all regions in Luzon with ten registered medicine brands, providing comprehensive pharmaceutical distribution.

Quality Products

Specializing in importation, wholesale distribution, promotion, and marketing of drugs and medicines to pharmaceutical establishments.
